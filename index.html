About Delightful Bean

Founded in 2020 by veteran film industry professionals, Delightful Bean emerged from a simple observation: quality coffee service can make or break a production day. Our founder, having spent 15 years in film production, understood firsthand the crucial role that reliable craft services play in maintaining crew morale and productivity.

Our mission is to elevate the film production experience through exceptional coffee service. We believe in fostering a positive on-set atmosphere, supporting sustainable coffee farming practices, and maintaining the highest standards of professionalism. Our core values of reliability, quality, and adaptability guide every interaction with our clients.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your standard service hours?

We arrive one hour before your first call time and maintain service until wrap. We accommodate both standard day shoots and night shoots with the same level of service.

How do you handle large crew sizes?

Our equipment and staff are scaled to your crew size. We can efficiently serve productions ranging from 20 to 200+ people with multiple service stations if needed.

What beverages do you offer besides coffee?

We provide a full range of options including specialty espresso drinks, various teas, hot chocolate, and cold brew. We can accommodate special requests and dietary restrictions.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance to ensure availability, though we can sometimes accommodate last-minute requests. Contact us for rush bookings.

What areas do you service?

We primarily serve the greater Lakeland area and can travel to locations within a 50-mile radius. Additional travel fees may apply for locations outside our primary service area.
